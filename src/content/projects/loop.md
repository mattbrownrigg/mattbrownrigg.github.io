LOOP is the data layer beneath a fully integrated research pipeline. 
A system that supports discretized literature, computational, experimental data 
for material prediction at every step. Every step is recorded as **dense
queryable embeddings** rather than loose files and notebooks.

The design favours reproducibility, any result can be traced
back through each step of the pipeline.
